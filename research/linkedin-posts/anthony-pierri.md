## Post 1 – Positioning
"Most SaaS homepages fail for one reason:
They describe what the product does, not what problem it solves.

'AI-powered project management platform' — nobody searches for this.
'Stop missing deadlines' — this is what your buyer feels.

Lead with the pain. Follow with the solution."

---

## Post 2 – ICP
"You don't have a content problem. You have an ICP problem.

If your LinkedIn posts aren't generating leads, ask yourself:
Is the person reading this my actual buyer?

Most B2B content is written for peers, not prospects.
Write for the person who has the problem you solve."

---

## Post 3 – Messaging Framework
"The best B2B SaaS messaging follows this structure:

1. Name the enemy (the problem/status quo)
2. Show the cost of inaction
3. Introduce your solution as the obvious fix
4. Prove it with specifics

Every LinkedIn post, every landing page, every cold email.
Same structure. Different execution."
