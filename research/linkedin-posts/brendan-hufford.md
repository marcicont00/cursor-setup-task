## Post 1
I've spent $45,572.77 of my own money on LinkedIn ads. Most people assume I'm an organic-only guy. Nope. I'm a win-with-content guy. I have one rule that most people skip: if a post can't earn attention organically, paying to distribute it just helps more people ignore it. Test organically. Scale with ads. Organic tells you what's worth amplifying. Ads make sure the right people keep hearing the same truth long enough that it becomes obvious. The average B2B buying journey is 272 days. You don't need one viral post. You need sustained presence with the right people.
