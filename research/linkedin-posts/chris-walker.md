## Post 1 – Dark Social
"60-80% of your B2B pipeline comes from channels you can't track.

Slack communities. Private DMs. Word of mouth. LinkedIn posts shared in emails.

This is dark social. And it's where most buying decisions actually happen.

Stop optimizing for what's measurable. Start optimizing for what's influential."

## Post 2 – Demand Generation
"Most B2B SaaS companies have a demand capture strategy, not a demand generation strategy.

Demand capture: Google Ads, SEO, retargeting — catching people already looking.
Demand generation: LinkedIn content, podcasts, communities — creating awareness before they search.

If you only do demand capture, you're fighting for the 3% ready to buy today.
Demand gen builds the other 97%."

## Post 3 – Content Strategy
"Your buyers are on LinkedIn every day.
