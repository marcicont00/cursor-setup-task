## Post 2
The human skills most rapidly declining in value: Memorizing information. Hustle, grinding, and overworking. Finding and following best practices. Execution without clarity. Collecting credentials to signal worth. Incremental optimization. These skills were most rewarded when information was scarce, systems were stable, and change was slow. The human skills that will be most valuable in the future: Clarity. Self-Trust. Discernment. Emotional Sovereignty. Intuition. Creativity. Connection. Adaptability. Autonomy. These are the skills that matter most when information is infinite, systems are unstable, and change is fast and constant.
