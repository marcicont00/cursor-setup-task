## Post 3 – Metrics That Matter
"Vanity metrics on LinkedIn:
- Impressions
- Likes
- Follower count

Real metrics:
- Profile visits from target accounts
- Connection requests from ICPs
- Inbound DMs from qualified prospects

Optimize for the second list."
