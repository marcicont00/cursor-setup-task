## Post 2 – Content System
"Most founders fail at LinkedIn not because of bad content.
But because they have no system.

My framework:
- Monday: Tactical post (how-to, framework)
- Wednesday: Story post (experience, lesson)
- Friday: Opinion post (contrarian take, hot topic)

Rotate the format. Stay consistent on the topic."

## Post 3 – Pipeline from Content
"I helped a SaaS founder go from 0 to $50k MRR using only LinkedIn organic.

No ads. No cold email. No SDRs.

The playbook:
1. Post 3x/week for 90 days
2. Engage deeply with every comment
3. DM everyone who engages — start a real conversation
4. Offer value before asking for anything

LinkedIn is a long game. But it compounds."
