# Melissa Kwan – LinkedIn Posts

## About
Co-founder and CEO of eWebinar. Documents her founder journey publicly on LinkedIn — a strong example of authentic B2B SaaS storytelling that builds trust and pipeline.

LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/melissakwan/

---

## Post 1 – Founder Authenticity
"I used to think I had to appear successful to attract customers.

Then I started writing honestly about the hard parts of building a SaaS company.
Revenue stagnation. Difficult co-founder conversations. Pivots that scared me.

My following tripled. My inbound leads doubled.

Authenticity is a distribution strategy."

---

## Post 2 – Building in Public
"Month 18 of building eWebinar.

What's working: Our retention is 94%. Customers love the product.
What's not working: We haven't cracked top-of-funnel yet.

I share this not because it's comfortable, but because someone out there is in the same place.
You're not alone."

---

## Post 3 – SaaS Storytelling
"The best LinkedIn content for B2B SaaS founders isn't about your product.

It's about:
- The problem you personally experienced before building it
- The customer who changed how you think about the market
- The decision that almost killed the company — and what you learned

Stories build trust. Trust builds pipeline."
