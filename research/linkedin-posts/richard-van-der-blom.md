## Post 1 – Algorithm Research
"After analyzing 9,000+ posts, here's what the LinkedIn algorithm rewards in 2024:

1. Dwell time — how long people stop on your post
2. Early engagement — likes/comments in first 60 min
3. Profile clicks — people visiting your profile after reading
4. Saves — underrated signal

Impressions are vanity. Saves and profile clicks are the real metrics."

## Post 2 – Best Format
"Document posts (carousels) get 3x more reach than text posts.

But here's what most people get wrong:
- Too many slides (keep it under 10)
- No clear takeaway on slide 1
- Forgetting a strong CTA on the last slide

Format matters less than clarity. But format still matters."

## Post 3 – Posting Time
"Best time to post on LinkedIn in 2024:
Tuesday–Thursday, 7–9am or 12–1pm (your audience's timezone).

But here's the truth: consistency beats timing.
Posting at the 'wrong' time every day beats posting at the 'right' time once a week."
