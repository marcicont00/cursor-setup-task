## Key Excerpts

"The fundamental problem with B2B marketing today is that everyone is optimizing for what they can measure, not for what actually drives revenue. MQLs, form fills, click-through rates — these are proxies. They are not revenue."

"When we survey buyers at the end of a deal and ask 'how did you first hear about us?' — the answer is almost never Google Ads or a gated ebook. It's 'I saw your CEO on LinkedIn' or 'someone in my network mentioned you in a Slack group.' That's dark social. That's where the real influence happens."

"The companies winning in B2B SaaS right now have one thing in common: they have a person — usually a founder or a senior operator — who creates content consistently and builds genuine trust with their target market. That person becomes the brand."

"LinkedIn organic is the highest ROI channel we've seen across 200+ B2B SaaS companies. Not because it's easy. Because it compounds. Every post you publish today is still working for you 6 months from now."
