# Justin Welsh – YouTube Transcript

## Source
Channel: Justin Welsh
Video: "How I Built a $5M Business With LinkedIn Organic Content"
URL: https://www.youtube.com/@JustinWelsh

## Key Excerpts

"I was a VP of Sales at a SaaS company. I burned out. I left. And I had no idea what I was going to do next. So I started writing on LinkedIn about what I knew — B2B sales, SaaS growth, building teams. I had no strategy. I just started."

"The first 90 days nothing happened. I'm talking 50 views per post. But I kept going because I noticed something: the people engaging with my content were exactly the people I wanted to work with. Quality over quantity, always."

"My entire content system runs on one principle: one core idea, broken into five angles. I pick one topic I know deeply — LinkedIn growth, solopreneur business, content systems — and I write about it from five different angles. That's my whole strategy."

"People ask me: what's the secret to LinkedIn? There is no secret. Post consistently. Write for one specific person. Be genuinely useful. Do that for 12 months and your life will change."

## Key Themes
- Starting from zero with no audience
- Quality engagement over follower count
- Content system built around core expertise
- Consistency as the only real strategy
